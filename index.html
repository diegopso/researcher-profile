About Me

April, 2016

I am a masters candidate in the Computing Institute at UNICAMP where I am engaged in research activities in the Computer Networks Lab. Also I am graduted in Information Systems in the ULBRA University and undergraduate student of Electrical Engineering at Federal University of Tocantins. Finally, I already lived in UK for a one and a half year for interchange studies in Electronic Engineering at the University of Portsmouth.

My research interests involve a range of topics, like...
